Solid and natural cosmetics: Free yourself from plastic packaging in your bathroom

Cristina Verde | April 28, 2024 | 5 min read

The bathroom is usually the space in the house that accumulates the most single-use plastic containers. Switching to solid cosmetics is an elegant and effective solution.

Solid shampoos, conditioners, and soaps not only eliminate plastic, but are often formulated with pure botanical ingredients free of harsh sulfates.

“

Taking care of your skin and hair with clean ingredients is also taking care of rivers and oceans.

A single solid shampoo bar can be equivalent to two or three liquid plastic bottles, lasting much longer and taking up less space when traveling.

1. Transition and adaptation

Your hair may need a couple of weeks to adapt to the absence of synthetic silicones. Be patient, the long-term result is totally worth it.
