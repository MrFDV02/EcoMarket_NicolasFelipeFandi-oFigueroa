Urban gardening in small spaces: Grow your own herbs

Sofía Gómez | May 2, 2024 | 4 min read

Having a small home garden is possible regardless of the square meters you have. A windowsill or a sunny corner on the balcony is enough.

Growing your own aromatic herbs reduces plastic packaging waste from the supermarket and connects you directly with nature.

“

Taking care of a plant is the purest exercise of patience and love that you can give yourself every day at home.

Start with resistant species like basil, mint, or rosemary. They require little care and bring incredible freshness to your meals.

1. Essential light and drainage

Make sure your pots have drainage holes and receive at least 4 or 6 hours of direct sunlight a day for optimal growth.
