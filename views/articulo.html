10 sustainable habits you can adopt today to reduce your environmental footprint

Laura Verde | May 15, 2024 | 6 min read

Small daily actions can generate big changes. Adopting sustainable habits not only benefits the planet, but also your health and your economy.

Here are 10 practical habits you can start today to reduce your environmental footprint and build a greener future.

“

Sustainability is not about perfection, but constant progress. Every small action counts.

Incorporating conscious changes into your daily routine may seem difficult at first, but with consistency it becomes natural and rewarding. Start small and watch your positive impact grow over time.

1. Reduce plastic consumption

Opt for reusable products like bottles, bags, and containers. Avoid single-use plastics and choose durable, recyclable alternatives whenever possible.

2. Buy local and in bulk

Support local producers and buy in bulk to reduce unnecessary packaging and transport emissions. Plus, you get fresher, better quality products.
